Hi there! I'm Anvi, a passionate developer and entertainment enthusiast who believes that managing your viewing experience should be as enjoyable as the content itself. MovieVault was born from my personal frustration with scattered watchlists, forgotten shows, and the constant question: "What should I watch next?"

As someone who loves both technology and storytelling, I wanted to create something that combines the precision of modern software with the joy of discovering great entertainment. Every feature in MovieVault reflects my own viewing habits and the challenges I've faced as a dedicated binge-watcher.
